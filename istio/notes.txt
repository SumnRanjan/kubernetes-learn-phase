SERVICE MESH & ISTIO – SIMPLE NOTES (Q/A + COMMAND STYLE)

---

1. WHAT IS SERVICE MESH?

---

Q: What is Service Mesh?
A: It is a layer that manages communication between microservices.

Q: What is the main idea?
A: Kubernetes allows communication, Service Mesh controls HOW it happens.

Example:
Pod A → Pod B
Pod A → Proxy → Pod B Proxy → Pod B

---

2. WHY SERVICE MESH IS USED?

---

Q: Why do we use Service Mesh?
A:

* Security (mTLS)
* Traffic management
* Observability
* Reliability

---

3. WHAT IS ISTIO?

---

Q: What is Istio?
A: It is a Service Mesh used in Kubernetes.

Q: How does it work?
A: Uses sidecar proxy (Envoy)

---

4. ISTIO ARCHITECTURE

---

Q: What are Istio components?

A:
Data Plane → Envoy proxies (handle traffic)
Control Plane → Istiod (manage config)

---

5. SIDECAR PROXY

---

Q: What is sidecar proxy?
A: A container that runs with the app and intercepts traffic.

Pod:

* App container
* istio-proxy

---

6. HOW ISTIO WORKS

---

Q: How Istio works?
A:

1. App sends request
2. iptables redirects to Envoy
3. Envoy applies rules
4. Request goes to destination Envoy
5. Then to service

---

7. ISTIO FEATURES

---

Q: What features Istio provides?

A:
Security → mTLS
Traffic → Load balancing, Canary
Reliability → Retry, Timeout
Observability → Metrics, Logs, Tracing

---

8. ISTIO COMPONENTS

---

Q: Important components?

A:
Envoy → Proxy
Istiod → Control plane
Gateway → Entry point
VirtualService → Routing
DestinationRule → Policies

---

9. COMMANDS

---

Check version:
istioctl version

Install Istio:
istioctl install --set profile=demo -y

Check pods:
kubectl get pods -n istio-system

Enable injection:
kubectl label namespace demo istio-injection=enabled

---

10. ISTIO VS KUBERNETES

---

Q: Difference?

Kubernetes → Enables communication
Istio → Controls communication

---

11. WHEN TO USE ISTIO

---

Q: When to use?
A: Microservices, production systems

Q: When not to use?
A: Small projects

---

12. INTERVIEW QUESTIONS

---

Q: What is Service Mesh?
A: Manages service communication

Q: What is Istio?
A: Service mesh using sidecar proxy

Q: What is sidecar proxy?
A: Helper container for traffic

Q: Does Istio enable communication?
A: No, Kubernetes does

Q: What is Envoy?
A: Proxy used in Istio

---

13. SUMMARY

---

Service Mesh → controls communication
Istio → implements using Envoy proxies
